What is a Home Warranty?

A home warranty is a service contract that covers repair and replacement costs when your home’s systems and appliances break down due to normal wear and tear. Liberty Home Guard oversees the process of repairing your home equipment from start to finish. Our network of state-licensed technicians ensures you have access to the highest quality workmanship and technical expertise.

A home warranty is an excellent complement to standard homeowner’s insurance. Insurance typically covers damages resulting from disaster, accident, or theft. It does not cover appliance breakdowns, home system repair, or home maintenance services—such coverage is the domain of home warranties. A homeowner with both a home warranty and home insurance policy enjoys more thorough and robust financial protection.

A home warranty is especially useful when a big-ticket item, such as a furnace or refrigerator, breaks down. Without financial protection, such major appliances can cost several hundreds or thousands of dollars to repair. A home warranty protects you from such exorbitant out-of-pocket expenses. A Liberty Home Guard plan can be yours for as little as $1 per day.
